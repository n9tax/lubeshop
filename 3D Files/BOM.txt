 The Lube Shop — Greaseweazle Drive Tower : BILL OF MATERIALS

 For the enclosure printed from weazleEnclosure.scad + greaseweasleShelf.scad.
 Quantities below are for the reference build: 2x 5.25" + 1x 3.5" drive.
 Fastener counts marked "per drive" scale if you change the `bays` stack.

 Fastener sizes/lengths are derived from the model and are a STARTING POINT —
 confirm against your actual drives, PSU, and inlet before ordering.

 Drive mounting screws        M3 x 8-10          4 per drive

   (2 side + 2 top, into the RD-50A-VP's tapped holes.)

   (Nuts drop into the hex pockets behind the flange; screw from the front.)

 Shelf-to-tower screws        M3 or M4 x 12      2
   (Top-rear of the tower; Ø4.2 clearance holes.)


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 NOTES
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 * The tower keeps a 5.25" footprint width whatever drives you fit; 3.5" drives
   sit within that width with their screw counterbores reaching the outer wall.
 * If you change the drive stack (the `bays = [...]` line in the .scad), only
   the drives and their mounting screws change — the electronics BOM is fixed.
 * MAINS WIRING IS DANGEROUS. Wire the IEC inlet, switch, fuse, and PSU to code;
   verify earth/ground continuity to any exposed metal before powering on.
